Tableau Two

Task:
Create a self-portrait by opening and rearranging windows on computer.Then take a screenshot.Pay attention to the content of your windows, the programs you use as well as the formal arrangement of your windows.Be expressive, not literal.

Explanation:
I created this desktop arrangement of the many, many screens I had open on my laptop at the time tosymbolize how my laptop screen looks most of the time - I am somebody who often focuses on multiple tasks at a time, and finds it hard to close applications / tabs while on the computer. I also tend to feel very overwhelmed with balancing volunteer work, a part time job, and course work at school, so my laptop is often super cluttered with current projects I'm working on.
